About Me

  • View Resume (PDF)

Hello! My name is Christopher Halim. I'm currently a master's student in Statistics at Columbia University in New York City. I started the program in the fall 2021 semester and am on track to graduate in the spring 2022 semester. Prior to Columbia, I completed my undergraduate degree at the University of Wisconsin-Madison, where I obtained by Bachelors degree in Business Administration majoring in Actuarial Science and Risk Management and Insurance

I am an agile problem-solver, intellectually curious, and growth-oriented. With my huge passion for data science and data analytics, I have collected more than 2 years of experience in the field. I have experience working with several data analysis tools, such as R, Tableau, Python (Jupyter Notebook, Matplotlib, Numpy, Pandas), SQL (MySQL, PostgreSQL), Microsoft Office (Excel, PowerPoint, Word), IBM Cognos, and SAS.

My diverse experience includes but not limited to working at a Fortune 500 insurance company as a pricing analyst, assisting data analysis research at a non-profit organization, and getting involve in a research initiative program to gain valuable research expertise
